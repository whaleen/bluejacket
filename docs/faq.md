# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Common questions about the warehouse and systems.

## Technical Support Questions

**Q: What's the WiFi password?**
A: Contact your IT department or supervisor for WiFi credentials.

**Q: How do I reset my password?**
A: Contact your IT department or use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

**Q: The app isn't working. What do I do?**
A: Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If issues persist, contact IT support.

## Warehouse Information

**Q: What are the warehouse hours?**
A: Contact your supervisor or check your employee handbook for current operating hours.

**Q: Where is the warehouse located?**
A: Refer to your company directory or contact your supervisor for warehouse location information.

**Q: Who do I call if I have a problem?**
A: Contact your direct supervisor or the warehouse manager for operational issues.

## Access & Permissions

**Q: How do I get access to the Warehouse app?**
A: Your supervisor needs to create an account for you in the system. Contact them to request access.

**Q: How do I add a new team member?**

**Q: I don't have permission to access something. What do I do?**
A: Contact your supervisor or the system administrator to request the necessary permissions.

## System Integration

**Q: How do I export data to Excel?**
A: Most reports in the Warehouse app and GE DMS have export buttons. Look for "Export" or "Download" options on the page you're viewing.

**Q: Can I integrate this with our other systems?**
A: Contact your IT department to discuss system integrations and API access.

**Q: How often does data sync from GE DMS?**
A: The GE DMS sync runs automatically every hour. Contact IT if you need manual sync.

## Company Policies

**Q: What's the return policy?**
A: Refer to your company's operations manual or contact your supervisor for return policies.

**Q: How do I handle damaged goods?**
A: Follow your company's damaged goods procedure. Contact your supervisor for specific instructions.

**Q: What do I do in an emergency?**
A: Follow your company's emergency procedures. Contact 911 for immediate emergencies, then notify your supervisor.
